Getting Started

It is essential for you to follow the following layout so our system process it correctly.

There are two things of note: the address and the phone number of the restaurant.

The Address of the Restaurant

The address of the restaurant is of this format:

{address number} {Street Name}, {suburb name} {state name} {postcode}

Example: It's Time for Thai Kingsford

The address is: 2/309 Anzac Parade, Kingsford NSW 2032

The Phone Number of the Restaurant

Please apply the following:

  • DO NOT enter the area code
  • All landline phone numbers (restaurants don't use their phone numbers) have a 8 digit number
